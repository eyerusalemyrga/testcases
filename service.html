Our Services

Pick-up

We give pick up services. Traders request pick up from our schedule, which has different dates and days for different locations.

Payment

Receive payments and rewards when you sell clothes. Payment is based on material and quantity. Traders are rewarded when they reach a minimum of 500 points.

Recycling

Recycling is our main purpose. This prevents the environment from being polluted, enhances clean living, and turns unsold clothes into profit for traders.
